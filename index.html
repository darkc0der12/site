What is Adsense?

Adsense is a totally free online platform from google, which enables you to earn money from home online. When you create content online like blog, websites using your skills and knowledge, you gain visitors (traffic). Simply those visitors can be the source of earnings for you. Or if you got a website or youtube channel with good traffic then adsense can show ads on your sites or videos, and in return it will share a portion of its earning with you.

What is Youtube?

Youtube is also a free platform from google where you can upload videos and make them available for the world. You can upload videos and gain subscriber and views. Adsense can be integrated with youtube, that means you can earn money online for your youtube videos.
